The people

Group Members

A small, close-knit group spanning undergraduate researchers to postdocs — with a long track record of training women in electrochemical biosensing.

Principal Investigator

DK

Dr. Damira Kanayeva

Associate Professor · Department of Biology, Nazarbayev University

Ph.D. in Cell and Molecular Biology, University of Arkansas. Research focuses on EIS aptasensors for cancer and infectious disease diagnostics. Approximately USD 1.6M in competitive funding as PI/Co-PI; four PhD graduates supervised to date, with three ongoing.
